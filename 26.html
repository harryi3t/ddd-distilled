Domain Experts and Business Drivers
There may be strong, or at least subtle, hints communicated by business stakeholders that could have
been used to help the technical team make better modeling choices. Thus, a
Big Ball of Mud
is often
the result of unbridled effort made by a team of software developers who don’t listen to the business
experts.
The business’s department or work group divisions can provide a good indication of where model
boundaries should exist. You will tend to find at least one business expert per business function.
