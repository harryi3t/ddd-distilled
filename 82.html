Domain
Events
are published by an
Aggregate
and subscribed to by an interested
Bounded Context.
The
messaging mechanism delivers the
Domain Events
to interested parties by means of subscriptions.
The interested
Bounded Context
can be the same one from which the
Domain Event
was published,
or it could be different
Bounded Contexts.
It’s just that in the case of the
BacklogItem
Aggregate
and the
Sprint
Aggregate
, the
publisher and subscriber are in the same
Bounded Context.
