Since instances of these two
Aggregates
are designed to be modified in separate transactions, how
do we get the instance of
Aggregate Type 2
updated based on changes made to the instance of
Aggregate Type 1
, to which our domain model must react? That’s a good question; we will
consider the answer to it a bit later in this chapter.
The main point to remember from this section is that business rules are the drivers for determining
what must be whole, complete, and consistent at the end of a single transaction.
