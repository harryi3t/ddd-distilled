• A

be located under the
Domain Event
that happens at the same time. So, you use vertical space
to represent parallel processing.
• As you go through this part of the storming session, you are going to find trouble spots in your
existing or new business process. Clearly mark these with a purple/red sticky note and some
text that explains why it’s a problem. You need to invest time at such points to learn more.
