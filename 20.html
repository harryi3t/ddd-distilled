The Learning Process and Refining Knowledge
DDD teaches a way of thinking to help you and your team refine knowledge as you learn about your
business’s core competencies. This learning process is a matter of discovery through group
conversation and experimentation. By questioning the status quo and challenging your assumptions
about your software model, you will learn much, and this all-important knowledge acquisition will
spread across the whole team. This is a critical investment in your business and team. The goal
should be not only to learn and refine, but to learn and refine as quickly as possible. There are
additional tools to help with those goals that can be found in
Chapter 7
, “

Management Tools
