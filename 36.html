Challenge and Unify
Now back to the question “What is core?” Using the previously out-of-control and ever-expanding
model, let’s challenge and unify!
One very simple challenge is to ask whether each of the large-model concepts adheres to the
Ubiquitous Language
of Scrum. Well, do they? For example,
Tenant
,
User
, and
Permission
have nothing to do with Scrum. These concepts should be factored out of our Scrum software model.
